Why Mobile Car Detailing in San Diego?

San Diego’s coastal climate — bright sun, ocean salt, and occasional marine layer — places unique demands on vehicle maintenance. Professional mobile car detailing brings the shop to you so that your car receives high-quality cleaning, paint protection, and interior care without interrupting your day. Technicians arrive with professional equipment and products tailored to protect paint, trim, glass, and upholstery.

For trusted, on-site service, consider Primos Auto Mobile Car Detailing for industry-standard techniques delivered at your home or workplace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does each service take?
A: Most standard packages take 1–3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. Ceramic coating and paint correction may require half a day and return visits due to curing times.

Q: Do you bring water and power?
A: Technicians typically bring all necessary supplies but may request access to an outdoor spigot or power outlet for some services — this will be noted during booking.

Q: Is the Ceramic Coating guaranteed?
A: Ceramic coatings come with care instructions and expected longevity. Warranty terms vary by product; ask for specifics when booking.

Recommended Care Schedule

  • Exterior Wash + Interior Detail: every 2–4 weeks for daily drivers.
  • Wash and Wax: every 2–3 months to maintain gloss and protection.
  • Complete Detail: every 6–12 months depending on exposure and condition.
  • Ceramic Coating: lasts 2–5 years depending on product and maintenance; annual inspections recommended.
